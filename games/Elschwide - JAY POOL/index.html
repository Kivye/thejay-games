How to Play JAY POOL

8-Ball rules. Pot your group (solids 1-7 or stripes 9-15), then sink the 8-ball to win. Avoid fouling!

Controls:
• Click & drag from cue ball to aim
• Release to shoot (longer = more power)
• Right-click for fine aim adjustment

Fouls:
• Cue ball pocketed (scratch)
• Wrong ball hit first
• 8-ball pocketed before your group is cleared
